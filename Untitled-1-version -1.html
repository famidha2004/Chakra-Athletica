Beginner page

Beginner? Perfect.

Most people who walk into Chakra have never tried spin before, so you're not alone. We guide you through every movement, every beat, every breath.

  • You decide your pace. You control your resistance. You listen to your body.
  • No pressure. No judgment. No competition.
  • Just you, your bike, the music and the possibility of feeling something shift inside you.
Every Body is Welcome

Your body is the right body.

Tall, short, big, small, flexible, stiff, nervous, excited. It doesn't matter. Our instructors help you set up your bike so you feel comfortable and supported.

  • You'll move safely, build confidence, and discover strength you forgot or never knew existed.
  • Socks, water bottle, and athletic clothes are all you need to bring.
  • Please arrive 15 minutes before class. Late entry is not permitted at Chakra.
